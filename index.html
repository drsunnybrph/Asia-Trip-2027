Afternoon
🎒 Shimokitazawa browse

Biggest electronics + watch selection in Japan.
Evening

Morning
🌊 TeamLab Borderless (Azabudai Hills) Pre-booked
Full maze · wander anywhere · nothing like Planets. She will love this more than anything on the trip.
Midday
🔪 Kappabashi Kitchen Town — knife shopping
10 min walk from TeamLab. Go to KAMA-ASA — free hand engraving, tax-free for tourists. Budget ~$600–900 for a 3-knife lifetime set (Gyuto + Nakiri + Petty). Knives go in checked luggage on May 1.
Afternoon
⚡ Akihabara — Hey Arcade · Mandarake · sensory overload
10 min walk from Kappabashi. Hey Arcade for fighters + rhythm games · Mandarake for rare figures. 2–3 hrs.
Evening
🍣 Sushi no Midori Splurge
Not overrated · not $400pp · ~$50–70pp · go at open to beat the line.
Morning
☕🍫 Matcha + chocolate haul

Midday
🛒 Don Quijote Shinjuku chaos 😂
Snacks · gadgets · costumes · $3 sake. Set a budget or it's over.
Afternoon
🎮 MariCAR street karting Pre-booked
Costumes · Shinjuku/Shibuya streets · ~1.5–2 hrs.
