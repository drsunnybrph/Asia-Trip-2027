Afternoon
🛬 Land ICN · check in · walk Hongdae

Evening
🥩 First KBBQ — samgyeopsal + soju
Find a packed spot near the hotel. Initiation night.
Tue · Apr 13
🏯 Culture Day
▾
Morning
🏯 Gyeongbokgung Palace + 🏘️ Bukchon Hanok Village
One cultural anchor for the whole trip. Great photos. She'll love the hanok streets — wear comfortable shoes.
Afternoon
🧧 Insadong → 🛍️ Myeongdong street food crawl
Tteokbokki · egg bread · honey chips. Tourist-friendly but genuinely good.
Wed · Apr 14
🌊 Chill Seoul
▾
Morning
🏙️ Dongdaemun Design Plaza (DDP)
Architectural flex. Good for photos, cool market nearby.
Afternoon
🌊 Han River picnic — GS25 snacks + rent bikes
Classic Seoul couple activity. Yeouido or Banpo park.
Evening
🍽️ Cross to Gangnam for dinner
Different energy than Hongdae — more polished.
Thu · Apr 15
👸 Her Day
▾
All Day
👜 Apgujeong Rodeo + 🌿 Garosu-gil boutiques
Upscale shopping, incredible cafés. She will be in her element. Full day here is easy.
Evening
🛁 Dragon Hill Jimjilbang spa night
Mixed common areas + gender-separated baths. ~$20pp. Unique experience.
Fri · Apr 16
🎶 Last Seoul Night
▾
Daytime
🛍️ Hongdae final shopping run
Streetwear · Korean beauty · accessories. Last Korea shopping day.
Evening
🥩 Last KBBQ + 🍺 Hongdae bar hop
Seoul's best nightlife is in Hongdae. Stay as late as you want — KTX isn't until tomorrow.

Busan
Apr 17 – 21 · 4 nights · Haeundae/Gwangalli Airbnb
Fresh seafood, beaches, more chill than Seoul
Sat · Apr 17
🌊 Arrive + Gwangalli Night
▾
Afternoon
Check in, drop bags, walk to Gwangalli Beach
Seafood dinner right on the beach strip.
Evening
🌉 Gwangan Bridge lit up at night + rooftop drink
The bridge lit at night is genuinely beautiful. Great first Busan impression.
Sun · Apr 18
🛕 Temple + Beach
▾
Morning
🛕 Haedong Yonggungsa Temple
Clifftop coastal temple — one of the most unique in Korea. Go early before crowds. 30 min from Haeundae.
Afternoon
🏖️ Haeundae Beach walk + 🐟 fresh hoe lunch
Not swimming weather in April but walkable and scenic. Fresh raw fish nearby.
Mon · Apr 19
🎨 Culture + Market
▾
Morning
🎨 Gamcheon Culture Village
Colorful hillside village with murals. Half day, great photos. She'll love this.
Afternoon
🐠 Jagalchi Fish Market
Buy fresh seafood upstairs and get it cooked right there. Wild experience.
Tue · Apr 20
🛍️ Last Busan Day
▾
Daytime
🏬 Centum City / Shinsegae
One of the largest malls in the world. Good for last Korea shopping.
Evening
🌉 Gwangalli final dinner + drinks
Last night in Korea. Pork belly + soju. Go out right.

▾

Tokyo
Apr 27 – May 1 · 4 nights · Shinjuku
Energy, cars, shopping, food, the final boss
One splurge meal a day
Apr 28 🥩 A5 Wagyu yakiniku
Apr 29 🍣 Sushi no Midori
Apr 30 🍜 Ichiran (ritual)
Tue · Apr 27
✈️ Arrive · Piss Alley · Golden Gai
▾
Afternoon
Check in Shinjuku · drop bags · decompress
Evening
🍢 Omoide Yokocho — sit down and actually eat
Tiny stool · full yakitori sets · cold Sapporo · smoky alley energy. Don't just walk through.
Late Night
🍸 Golden Gai bar hop
100+ tiny themed bars, 6 seats each, cash only. Pick the weirdest ones. Most memorable night in Tokyo. 🔥
Wed · Apr 28
⛩️ Meiji · Burger · Shimokita · BIC · Wagyu
▾
Morning
⛩️ Meiji Jingu — only shrine you need
Forested, massive torii gate, dead quiet. Go early. 45–60 min total.
Lunch
🍔 Shogun Burger — best in Tokyo
Wagyu smash burger. Consistently top-rated.
Afternoon
🎒 Shimokitazawa browse
Vintage shops · indie cafés · zero tourist energy. She'll love it. 1–2 hrs easy.
Late PM
⌚ BIC Camera Shinjuku — Seiko hunting
Biggest electronics + watch selection in Japan.
Evening
🥩 A5 Wagyu yakiniku splurge
Yoroniku or Yakiniku Jumbo Hanare. Grill at the table. Skip "Kobe" label — A5 grade is the real flex. ~$150–250 for 2.
Thu · Apr 29
🌊 TeamLab · Akihabara · Sushi  Showa Day
▾
Morning
🌊 TeamLab Borderless — go at open pre-booked
Azabudai Hills. Full maze, wander anywhere. Nothing like Planets. National holiday — go first thing. She will love this more than anything on the trip. 🥹
Afternoon
⚡ Akihabara — Hey Arcade · Mandarake · sensory overload
Evening
🍣 Sushi no Midori splurge
Not overrated. Not $400pp. ~$50–70pp. Go at open to beat the line. Genuinely excellent.
Fri · Apr 30
🛒 Donki · Matcha · MariCAR · Ichiran · Daikoku
▾
Morning
☕🍫 Matcha + chocolate haul
Ippodo Tea — ceremonial matcha + bamboo whisk + stand. Tokyo Station — Royce · Press Butter Sand · NewYork Perfect Cheese.
Midday
🛒 Don Quijote Shinjuku chaos
Snacks · gadgets · costumes · $3 sake. Set a budget or it's over. 😂
Afternoon
🎮 MariCAR street karting pre-booked
Costumes · Shinjuku/Shibuya streets · ~1.5–2 hrs.
Evening
🍜 Ichiran solo booth ramen ritual
Late Night
🚗 Daikoku Futo PA — 10pm+
Uber to Yokohama ~30–40 min. JDM everything. Friday = best turnout. Stay as long as you want. Best car night of your life. 🚗💨
Sat · May 1
🌃 Shibuya Crossing · Fly Home ✈️
▾
Morning
Pack · check out
Late AM
🌃 Shibuya Crossing — stand in the middle
Iconic. Takes 10 min. Do it once. Then grab last food nearby.
Afternoon
🏪 Last konbini run at HND · ✈️ HND → SFO
7-Eleven or Lawson · onigiri 🍙 · Pocky · drinks for the flight. See you on the other side. 🌊
